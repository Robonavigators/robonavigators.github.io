Retro_Console

This project supports Over-The-Air updates. Ensure your device is powered on, connected to your Wi-Fi, and you know its local IP address.

  • Optimized for full white 1.3inch SH1106 OLED
  • Mimics old retro consoles
  • Uses modern features
Note: Your computer and the ESP32 must be connected to the same Wi-Fi network for OTA to work.
For more information visit Robonavigators Github
Ensure OTA routing is enabled in your device firmware
