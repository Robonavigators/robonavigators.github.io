Erase Flash Memory

Connect your ESP32/ESP8266 board via USB. This tool will completely format the flash memory, removing all partitions, firmware, and stored Wi-Fi credentials.

Destructive Action

This process cannot be undone. Your board will be rendered unbootable until new firmware is flashed.
