Desk Buddy

Installs the interactive Desk Buddy project. Ensure your ESP32-C3 is connected via USB before starting.

Note: Requires a Chromium-based browser (Chrome, Edge, or Brave). Make sure the Arduino IDE or serial monitor is closed before flashing.
